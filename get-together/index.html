Get Together !

History of the club

In 1952 a group of friends opened a library in downtown Edinburgh. Every Friday night they met to discuss their current books. One day they had the idea to open their little meeting to other people such as their customers. So the first book club opened its doors a year later in 1953. After that the club never stopped growing.
And here we are today with the Get together club!
